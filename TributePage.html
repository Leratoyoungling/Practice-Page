Rapelang Rabana

Tech Entrepreneur and Chief Digital Officer of BCX


Here's a time line of Rapelang Rabana's life:

Born in Gaborone Botswana.

She graduated from University of Cape Town in 2005, with a bachelor's degree in Business Science.

Co-created the VoIP mobile application called Yeigo in 2007.

Created an entertainment website called Tomplaygo in 2012.

Started a company called Rekindle Learning, which focuses on online learning in 2013.

She was named a Chief Digital Officer of BCX in 2017.


Achievements

2012- She was on Oprah Magazine's O Power list.

2013- She was listed on Forbes's 30 under 30 list of best entrepreneurs of Africa.

2014- She was named one of the Entrepreneurs of the world by World Entrepreneurship Forum.
